Core capabilities

Marine workshop and repair services

Capabilities are grouped the way technical managers and superintendents typically scope work—from machinery and deck systems through piping, steel, electrical, and propulsion.

Engine and machinery

  • Main engine troubleshooting and overhaul support
  • Auxiliary engine and generator servicing
  • Turbocharger, purifier, compressor, and boiler work
  • Pump overhaul, alignment, and heat exchanger servicing

Deck and cargo equipment

  • Crane, winch, windlass, and mooring equipment repair
  • Hydraulic deck machinery troubleshooting
  • Hatch cover maintenance and cargo gear support
  • Lifting gear inspection support and corrective works

Piping and fabrication

  • Pipe renewal for ballast, bilge, fire, steam, fuel, and lube systems
  • Hydraulic piping repair and pressure testing
  • Valve overhaul, flange work, and gasket renewal
  • Custom steel supports, brackets, and foundations

Steel and welding

  • Steel renewal, plate replacement, and deck repairs
  • Bulkhead, stiffener, and structural reinforcement works
  • MIG, TIG, arc, stainless, aluminum, and repair welding
  • Emergency hot work and workshop fabrication jobs

Electrical and automation

  • Switchboards, starters, motors, panels, and cable replacement
  • Alarm and monitoring system troubleshooting
  • Instrumentation checks for pressure, temperature, flow, and level
  • PLC and control circuit fault support

Pumps, valves, propulsion

  • Ballast, bilge, fire, freshwater, sludge, and cargo pump work
  • Valve lapping, overhaul, and replacement support
  • Steering gear, rudder, coupling, and shaft line support
  • Operational testing after repair and reporting

Spare parts and stores

Supply support alongside repair work

Hands-on repair is backed by sourcing and logistics so the vessel can sail with the parts and stores required for the next leg.

Spare parts

  • Main engine, auxiliary engine, and generator spares
  • Pump, valve, compressor, boiler, and turbocharger spares
  • Filters, bearings, seals, gaskets, couplings, and hoses
  • Electrical parts including motors, relays, breakers, and starters
  • OEM and compatible replacement parts, including urgent sourcing

Marine stores and consumables

  • Deck, engine, cabin, galley, and cleaning stores
  • PPE, workwear, ropes, lashing materials, paints, and tools
  • Lubricants, sealants, hoses, fasteners, and workshop consumables

  • Ship-side delivery coordination and consolidation support
